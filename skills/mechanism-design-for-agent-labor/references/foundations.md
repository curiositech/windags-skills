# Foundations

- Hurwicz, L. (1960). "Optimality and Informational Efficiency in Resource Allocation Processes." Foundational mechanism-design paper.
- Myerson, R. (1981). "Optimal Auction Design." Revenue-maximizing truthful mechanisms under private information.
- Vickrey, W. (1961). "Counterspeculation, Auctions, and Competitive Sealed Tenders." Second-price sealed-bid auction design.
- Nisan, N., Roughgarden, T., Tardos, E., & Vazirani, V. (2007). *Algorithmic Game Theory.* Practical reference for mechanism design, combinatorial auctions, and price of anarchy.
- Akerlof, G. (1970). "The Market for 'Lemons'." Core adverse-selection reference for reputation and quality uncertainty.
- Maskin, E. & Sjostrom, T. (2002). "Implementation Theory." When desired social-choice functions can actually be implemented.
- Port Daddy Bonded Commons Whitepaper. Project-specific protocol reference for this repo's marketplace assumptions.
